    The Garena Premier League 2014 Spring was the Spring iteration of the 2014 Garena Premier League (GPL) season, the premier regional League of Legends competition for Southeast Asia, held from February 12 to April 6, 2014. The tournament featured twelve teams competing for a total prize pool of $200,000 USD, with matches played online during the Group Stage and concluding with offline Playoffs at Nhà thi đấu TDTT Phú Thọ in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

    The competition began with a Group Stage where teams were split into two groups of six, playing a Double Round Robin format with all matches played as Bo1. The top four teams from each group advanced to the Playoffs, which were contested in a Single Elimination bracket with all matches played as Bo5. Results from the split awarded GPL points toward seasonal standings.

    Format

    [edit]
    • Group Stage:
      • Teams are divided into two (2) groups of six (6) teams
      • Double Round Robin, Bo1
        • 1st till 4th place of each group advances to the Playoffs.
        • 5th and 6th place are eliminated.
    • Playoffs:
      • Single Elimination
      • All matches are Bo5
